MOROCCAN TEA



Moroccan-Tea

LET US SPOIL YOU as we pour the finest mint tea in our sumptuous Tea Salon or INVIGORATE you with a traditional Baghrir, Amlou and Yoghurt breakfast in our shaded terrace. Perhaps its time to lunch from a delicate Harira soup and share a meat, seafood or vegetable Tagine.

OUR MENU

Our-Menu

The menu at House OF FRIENDS has been chosen with care by British chef, PAUL WEAVER. As a result of visiting a number of local vendors in the souks within the Medina — as well as organic farms — a SHARING CONCEPT was formed that is focused on local, seasonal and sustainable produce. House OF FRIENDS’s dishes aim to showcase the best Morocco has to offer, combining traditional cooking methods with a European influence and Mediterranean flair.
